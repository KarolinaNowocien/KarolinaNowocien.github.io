BIO.

Patrycja Michera was trained to work as an architect, when she discovered her love for makeup and fashion. Just after starting job in architectural industry, she decided to change the path of career and become a professional makeup artist.

Today Patrycja is cooperating with talented fashion and beauty photographers from Poland and abroad, working on many projects like editorials, campaigns, catalogues, look books, videos and advertisements.

Her work was published in magazines such us: Vogue.it, HIRO, Law Business Quality Magazine, MADAME, Be Magazyn, Glamour.pl and more.
